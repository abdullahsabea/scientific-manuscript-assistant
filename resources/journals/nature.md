# Nature Portfolio Guidelines

## AI Policy
Nature strictly regulates the use of LLMs.
- Large Language Models (LLMs) cannot be credited as an author.
- The use of LLMs must be documented in the Methods section (or Acknowledgements, if no Methods section is available).
- Authors are entirely responsible for the accuracy and originality of the work.

### Suggested Disclosure Template
> "The authors used [NAME TOOL / SERVICE] for [REASON, e.g., language editing/structural suggestions]. The authors take full responsibility for the final content and the original ideas presented."

## Formatting Preferences
- Abstracts for Nature are fully referenced and strictly ~150 words.
- Ensure all claims are strongly backed by empirical data.
- Avoid over-claiming; use precise language.
- Figure captions should begin with a brief title sentence describing the entire figure.
