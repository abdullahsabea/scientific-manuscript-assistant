# Additive Manufacturing (Elsevier) Guidelines

## Scope & Specificity
The *Additive Manufacturing* journal expects rigorous, reproducible reporting of process parameters.
- Always ensure the following parameters are explicitly stated or logically deduced:
  - Laser power (W)
  - Scan speed (mm/s)
  - Layer thickness (μm or mm)
  - Hatch spacing (μm or mm)
  - Volumetric Energy Density (VED) (J/mm³)
- For lattice structures/TPMS:
  - Clearly define the implicit surface equation or unit cell design.
  - State the relative density and strut/wall thickness.

## AI Policy
Follows the standard Elsevier AI policy:
- Disclose use of AI in a dedicated section or Acknowledgements.
- AI must only be used for readability/language.
